2024 is slowly shifting to dawn, it is soon time to kiss it goodbye and welcome 2025 with our arms wide open.

For me personally, 2024 has been one of the most impactful year ever. I'll describe a little bit about it here.

I'll start this new "series" of post for each year ending. This is very much inspired of [Frank Ruan](https://frank-ruan.com/)'s blog page, but I find the exercise of putting back your experience over the year very interesting.
This will help remember the actions I have taken over the year, and let others see my accomplishment and what I've gone through.

## Studies

Before attacking this part, here's a quick recap on how things worked out:

1. Regarding holidays...
    - The last week of July is our last week at school, representing a 2-months break for students.
    - Starting the first week in September, we would come back to school and start studies.

2. Regarding my diploma and apprenticeship...
    - I have started my studies last year, in Networking & Telecommunications.
    - This studies represent a 3-year engagement to obtain the diploma.
    - Starting our second year (By September 2024), we can enter school with an apprenticeship, which consist of obtaining a job, and shifting between "school-time" and "work-time".
    - Our shifts is generally represent by 1 month long shifts. (I will go to school for a month, then the next month, I will go to work).
